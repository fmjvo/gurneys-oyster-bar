05 — Guardrails

Do's & don'ts

Do

  • Keep everything on the dark slate ground
  • Use chalk-white (#F1EDE1), never pure white
  • Let letters bounce slightly off the baseline
  • Limit each piece to one or two brights
  • Keep body text in clean Inter for legibility

Don't

  • Put pink chalk on a light background
  • Set long paragraphs in the hand-drawn font
  • Crowd all three brights together
  • Use hard pure-black or sterile flat fills
  • Stretch or recolour the oyster mark
